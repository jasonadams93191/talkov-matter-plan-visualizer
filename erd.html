Naming traps — objects that do NOT exist

There is no litify_pm__Matter_Task__c (generated tasks are standard Task records), no litify_pm__Default_Matter_Plan__c and no litify_pm__Default_Matter_Stage__c (the template objects are litify_pm__Matter_Plan__c / litify_pm__Matter_Stage__c themselves), and no standalone litify_pm__Party__c object — Party is a lookup field on litify_pm__Role__c pointing to Account.

Stage switch mechanics

1) Repoint litify_pm__Matter__c.litify_pm__Matter_Stage_Activity__c to the target stage activity · 2) Record-triggered flow detects IsChanged, stamps Set_As_Active_At__c + Stage_Status__c · 3) Sweep still-open Tasks of other stages to Status = N/A · 4) Instantiate the target stage's litify_pm__Default_Matter_Task__c records as Tasks, owner resolved from the matter team by role · 5) Cross-plan moves land via litify_pm__Matter_Stage_Switch__c (plan + stage pair). Branch rules for partition: settle pre-suit (Stage 3 → 11), PRPA skip (5 → 6 or 7), buyout closes (6 → 11), decline/refer (2 → Closed: Withdrawn).
